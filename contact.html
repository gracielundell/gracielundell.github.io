Hi, I'm Gracie Lundell!

Portland-raised, I have been involved with frontend web development since September 2015. I am fascinated with the way users navigate the web and am driven to make their experiences more intuitive and unique. I believe there is power in designing the perfect user experience and understand the responsibility that comes with it. Through Sketch, Invision, HTML, Sass, and JavaScript, I want to create memorable experiences for users. I want to help people with incredible ideas catch the eyes of those who can help them change the world.

I am motivated and hard-working, constantly evolving and ever growing. When I'm not playing with shapes in Sketch, experimenting with CSS, or coding sweet JavaScript, I enjoy exploring nature, gardening, ceramics, and reading. I love to get my hands dirty in everything I do.
